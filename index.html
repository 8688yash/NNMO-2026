Introduction to NNMO 2026

India's Maritime Movement for Youth

The Naavik National Maritime Olympiad (NNMO) 2026 is a premier national-level competition aimed at igniting maritime awareness and showcasing career opportunities among school students of Classes 9 to 12. It combines education, adventure, and exposure to the world of seafaring and marine innovation.

Core Objectives

  • Promote awareness about maritime careers and the global shipping industry.
  • Enable students to explore unique career pathways in the maritime domain.
  • Provide a national platform for skill-based, logical, and maritime-themed competition.
  • Reward excellence through prizes, scholarships, and mentorship.

"Join us to chart a course for the next generation of India's maritime leaders."

Olympiad Structure & Syllabus

Olympiad Format

Round 1: Online Aptitude

Duration: 60 mins

Type: Maritime Aptitude & Awareness (MCQs)

Round 2: Advanced Test

Duration: 45 mins

Type: Case-based & Logical Thinking

Round 3: Grand Finale

Duration: ~20 mins

Type: Live Interview (Personality & Potential)

Comprehensive Syllabus

General Awareness (20%)

Indian & World Geography, Maritime History, Current Affairs, Blue Economy.

Career Aptitude (15%)

Maritime Career Paths, Skill Assessment, Top Institutions (IMU, etc.).

Logical Reasoning (20%)

Verbal, Quantitative, and Visual Reasoning with a maritime context.

Maritime Science & Tech (25%)

Applied Physics (Buoyancy), Chemistry (Corrosion), Marine Biology, Ship Tech.

Digital & Future Skills (10%)

IoT in Shipping, GPS, AI in Maritime, Cybersecurity basics.

Life Skills & English (20%)

Leadership, Ethics, Crisis Handling, Reading Comprehension, Nautical Terms.
